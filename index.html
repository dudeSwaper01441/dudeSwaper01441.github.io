How it works

Up and running in 3 steps

No accounts, no installs, no backend. Just load your data and start exploring.

1

Load your data

Paste a Google Sheets link, type rows manually, or load the built-in sample dataset to get started immediately.

2

Map your columns

Tell the app which column is dek, which is faculty, and so on. It auto-guesses common names.

3

Explore & compare

Click through charts, drill into faculties and programs, or jump to the Compare page to see year-over-year trends.
